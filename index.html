Bio / Electronic Press Kit

"If you are anything like me, you spend a lot of time imagining what would have happened if you had said what you truly wanted to say. I write songs to narrate these alternate realities that exist entirely inside my head." -Nel Abdiel

Nel Abdiel is a bilingual (Spanish/English) singer-songwriter from Puerto Rico, based in Washington DC. Nel's Acoustic Pop is heavily influenced, both lyrically and musically, by Rock En Español, and the Nova Trova musical movement of the Caribbean. Nel has studied Songwriting and Music Production at Berklee under the tutelage of music industry giants like Bonnie Hayes, Andrea Stolpe, and Chrissy Tignor, among others.

"No Es Para Ti" (It's not for you) is Nel's first official release. He wrote it after finding out that an ex had been telling people his songs were about them. Have you ever dated someone so self centered that even after being broken up for years, they still think they are the center of your universe? Well, this is the song you've been waiting for. The song you most definitely need to dedicate to them. The Schrodinger's song, it is both for them and it is not for them simultaneously.

If you are a member of the press send us a message via instagram or email at nel.abdiel (at) gmail.com and we'll get you access to our music prior to the official release for any write ups or features. Same for contact info for booking.
