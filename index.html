Bio

My first single, Growing Sunflowers, will be available in all streaming platforms on January 22nd 2021! Make sure to follow me on bandcamp to get notified!

Yo can also listen to my work in progress songs in the next section.

Nel is a singer/songwriter from Puerto Rico, based in Washington DC. He has studied Songwriting and Music Production at Berklee under the tutelage of music industry giants like Bonnie Hayes, Andrea Stolpe, and Chrissy Tignor, among others.
